About Eco Grow

Eco Grow is a smart agriculture platform that helps farmers and agricultural enthusiasts make informed decisions about crop selection, fertilizer usage, and plant disease detection. Our aim is to empower sustainable farming using technology, machine learning, and real-time data insights to improve yield and efficiency.
